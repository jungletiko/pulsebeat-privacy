# Privacy Policy for PulseBeat

**Effective Date:** 28 December 2025

PulseBeat ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

We may collect the following information:
- Basic profile information (such as name and profile picture) when you authenticate via Facebook, Instagram, or Threads
- Email address (if provided)
- App usage data and interaction logs
- Device and browser information

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
- Authenticate users
- Provide and maintain PulseBeat services
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information.

We may share data only:

## 4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.
